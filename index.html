Zapp Station

2023

User Research

Wire-framing

UI Design

Design System

Illustration Style

Prototype

Usability Testing

Zapp Station is an EV charging station finder app.

EV drivers were experiencing constant anxiety about charging availability. Research with 15+ users revealed the core issue:unreliable information leading to broken trustin the charging infrastructure. So Zapp Station is introduced to help riders for easily accessing the chargers and plan the long rides care free.

My Role

I led the project as both a Brand Designer and Product Designer.
Brand Identity / Logo Design

EV is the replacement for carbon emission vehicles or engines so it signifies the preservation of nature thats the preservation of greenery, so I have the idea in color by sticking to the mother nature color which is light green signs of green energy and friendly as well. Then for the type I created the custom logo font just fot Zapp and created in a slightly tilted manner with round minimal style signifies technology and motion of evolution. Also created the Logo mark or we call it Monogram, which is representing the landmark with Z on it and also a leaf like structure to dedicated greenery of environment.

Product UI/UX Designer

A human-first UI with soft interactions and focused flows Built-in design system to ensure consistency across screens Clean, distraction-free interface to help users act quickly and confidently Tested for mobile, smartwatch, and car dashboard environments

Challenges

No reliable charger info:

EV drivers often reach stations that are offline, full, or out of service.

No real-time updates:

Users have no live status they cant tell if a charger is working or available.

No trip planning around charging:

Long trips are stressful without knowing where and when to charge.

Missing station details:

Most apps dont show charger types, photos, or nearby amenities.

No tool for station owners:

Merchants couldnt easily list or update station info, leading to outdated data.

Solutions

Real-time map

Integration real time map of EV stations with live charger status.

Trip Planner

Introduced the trip planner according with charging stops along a selected route.

Vehicle onboarding

Add you EVs with dynamic, engaging UI so that the app can recognize your ev and set accordingly.

Solutions

Advanced filter

Addition of filter by charger type, distance, amenities and nearby location, favorites etc.

Subscription

Subscription plan has added so that for busy schedule there will be no delay and hassel free charging based charging access.

Merchant UI

We added the merchant side app interface as well for station owner so that they can update station info, photos, and amenities.

Process

Research & Discovery

User interviews, ride-alongs with taxi drivers, competitive analysis, and pain point mapping.

Solution Architecture

Feature prioritization, user flow design, and cross-platform experience mapping.

UI, Interaction and Design System

Intuitive interface for customers and station owners along with Component library creation with 50+ elements ensuring consistency across iOS, Android, and CarPlay.

Different interaction for different actions are created to maintain lively hood of the app and maintained the interaction consistency along with vehicle onboarding.

Testing & Iteration

sability testing, A/B testing of critical flows, and beta testing with target user group

Outcome

  • Zapp Station reduces charging anxiety and saves EV users time by providing clear, real-time, and reliable charging information.
  • Long-distance travel becomes easier and stress-free with the Trip Planner feature.
  • Merchants benefit by attracting users with verified, up-to-date station data creating a smarter EV ecosystem overall.
